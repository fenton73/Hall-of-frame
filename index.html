Who We Are

A studio built on craft & care.

Hall of Frame is Coventry’s go-to destination for bespoke picture framing. Based in Earlsdon, we work with individuals, businesses, and collectors to protect and present the things that matter most.

From a treasured family photograph to a signed sports shirt, we treat every piece as a unique commission — advising on materials, mounts, and finishes to get the result exactly right.

With personal consultations as standard, you’ll never feel rushed. We take the time, because the result is worth it.

What We Do

Our services.

Talk to Us
01

Bespoke Picture Framing

Custom frames made to measure for your artwork, prints, and photography. A huge range of mouldings, mounts, and glass options to suit any piece and any interior.

02

Canvas Stretching

Professional canvas stretching for artists and collectors. Tight, flat, and gallery-ready — preserving your canvas beautifully for years to come.

03

Sports Memorabilia

Signed shirts, programmes, boots, and match day memories — mounted and framed to last a lifetime. Display your passion properly, the way it deserves.

Mirrors

Custom-framed mirrors to complement any interior. Choose your style, size, and finish for something truly unique that transforms a room.

05

Mounting & Presentation

Professional mounting for certificates, maps, medals, photographs, and documents. UV-protective glass available to prevent fading over time.
